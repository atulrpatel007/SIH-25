2. What is a Jute Ribboner Machine?
A jute ribboner is a mechanical device used to strip off the bark or outer layer (which contains the fibre) from the jute stalk.
Instead of immersing the whole plant stem, ribboners allow farmers to:
Mechanically peel the bark (ribbons) from the stalk,
Ret the ribbons only, which requires much less water, and
Ret them on-site (in-situ) in small water bodies, pits, or tanks.
Thus, the machine prepares the material for efficient in-situ retting.
